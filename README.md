**PhonKit** is a lightweight phonetic toolkit for working with words and their sounds.

It helps developers analyze pronunciation and sound structure in text — including phonemes, syllables, rhymes, and phonetic similarity.

Perfect for:

* NLP projects
* poetry / lyric tools
* rhyme generators
* pronunciation analysis
* word games
* linguistics research

---

## Planned Features / Goals

* **Phoneme extraction**
  Convert words into phoneme sequences.

* **Syllable counting**
  Quickly determine syllable counts for words.

* **Rhyme detection**
  Find words that rhyme based on phonetic endings.

* **Phonetic similarity**
  Compare words based on pronunciation.

* **Fast and lightweight**
  Designed for efficient phonetic lookup and analysis.
